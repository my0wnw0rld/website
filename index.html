Creation of the MOW device

Can anyone hear me? Hello? ...My name is Stanley. If you are listening, let me tell you my story.
In my short but what feels like a long existence, I felt lonely, the world is different from what it used to be. Everyone that I knew is not here anymore.
I was born with a special gift, the gift of creation and invention and so, I decided to put an end to all this loneliness and quiet that the world has dived into. I went salvaging, searching for parts to what i called the MOW device, it was supposed to be ok, all I wanted was to build a parallel universe, a meta one but it went wrong, my calculations failed. A world is not one without life and thus I designed these creatures called Slimes but something reverted, instead of entering My Own World, these creatures ended up being released in  the real world.
I can't control them, they are hectic by essence, they adopted characteristics of my favorite cartoons, from the comic books that I read. Some are good, some might be evil? I can't tell. Are they going to help me? Or they will make me dive deep into a state of madness?

MOW NFT is a High quality art and community driven collection, with 7,007 unique NFT's on the Ethereum Blockchain, where holders have the ability to choose the fate of MOWs and the direction of the MOW comic book.

Roadmap
MOWprime

The MOWprime is an NFT that comes with a scarce supply that powers the governance aspect of the MOWverse. Allows holders to access a private channel where their participation is pivotal in the direction and critical decisions on the Lore and the Backstory of the comic. Holders of the MOWprime also get granted access to the whitelist and free access to the comic strip that explains the genesis of the MOW Heroes... and villains. Down the road MOWprime holders will have even more advantages in the community.

Phase 1 - Mint

We will give everyone the oportunity to be part of this story, and help us shape the MOWverse

Phase 1 - Cooperative NFT's with other collections

We will focus on bonding some real and strong partnerships with blue chip collections and other artists. With that in mind we have some Special Edition NFT's that we will release with that pourpose.

Phase 1 - MOW Dao

Phase 1 - Comic

Luis, our in-house artist, with tons of given proofs about his potential, alongside with our creative team and the MOWprime holders will deliver to the community, the first issue of the comic book that features our heroes. The comics will be released Quarterly and you may expect a humorous and action packed story.

Phase 2 - Community Launchpad

The MOWverse is also for creatives!. MOW is born from the love of art and we want to give the opportunity for other artists to collaborate with us and our community in order to bring fun and high quality collections to our ecosystem thus highlighting the benefits of being a holder. We will work as a Launchpad for the community members that want help to develop their NFT Project. A % of the profits of the New Projects will be destributed to the MOW Holders.

Phase 2 - Gen 2!

Every hero needs a villain, it is no different this time! We are creating a full villain collection after the GEN 1 mint. All GEN 1 holders will have priority access to the whitelist.
